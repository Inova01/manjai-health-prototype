Name and purpose

From manje to ManjAI

ManjAI is derived from the Haitian Creole word manje, meaning food or to eat, combined with AI. The prototype presents how artificial intelligence can support preliminary nutrition-risk screening before physical wearable deployment.

System architecture

From simulated sensing to smarter screening

The proposed wearable architecture remains part of the design. In this prototype, the full workflow is demonstrated through software simulation and dashboard feedback.

01

Simulated Child Record

Age, anthropometry, MUAC, temperature, heart rate, SpO2, and activity indicators.

AI Risk Model

Trained classifier estimates Normal, Moderate, or High preliminary risk.

03

Dashboard Alert

Results are shown with visual status, model confidence, and follow-up guidance.

Preliminary AI validation

Model evidence for Chapter 5

The experiment compares Logistic Regression, Random Forest, SVM, and MLP on a 5,000-record synthetic preliminary dataset. The selected model prioritizes at-risk recall for screening usefulness.
